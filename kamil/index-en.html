About me

I am a Civil Engineer with expertise in structural engineering. My interest in civil engineering began from an early age and further developed during my studies. I am a graduate of Gdansk University of Technology majoring in concrete structures. I have acquired a master of science degree in civil engineering and finished my studies with distinction, receiving a final grade of 5,5 (on a scale of 2 to 5,5) on my diploma. I graduated in the top 5% of University graduates in 2015. I have dealt with both work in supervision on construction sites and structural engineering. In my professional work I show reliability, attention to detail and responsibility.

My offer

I deliver services to structural design offices, with whom I cooperate as a freelancer mainly in the form of remote work. I provide services in the field of technical documentation including technical drawings, static analysis and design as well as FEM modeling. I offer preparation of technical documentation both in English or Polish. The process of structural design is carried out on my own equipment and licensed software. Technical drawings are made in CAD.
