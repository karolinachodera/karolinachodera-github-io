About me

I am a Civil Engineer with expertise in structural engineering. My interest in civil engineering began from my early ages, further developed during my studies. I am a graduate of Gdansk University of Technology, majoring in concrete structures. I have acquired a master of science degree in civil engineering and finished my studies with distinctions, receiving a final grade of 5,5 (in a scale of 5,5) on my diploma. I graduated from the top 5% of University graduates in 2015. I have dealt with both work in supervision on construction sites and in structural engineering. In my professional work I show reliability, attention to details and responsibility.

My offer

My company assist structural design offices looking for aid in their projects. I offer cooperation as a freelancer, in form of a remote work. I provide services in the field of technical documentation including technical drawings, static analysis and design, FEM modelling. I offer preparation of technical documentation both in English or Polish. The process of structural design in carried out on my own equipment and licensed software. Technical drawings are made in CAD.
